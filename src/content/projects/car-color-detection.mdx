## Objective

Detect vehicles and classify their most likely visible color in real-world scenes where lighting and reflections vary.

## Pipeline

1. Detect cars with a YOLO model.
2. Crop each detected region of interest.
3. Compute robust color features in HSV/LAB spaces.
4. Map extracted features to predefined color labels.

## Practical challenges

- Metallic paint and glare can shift color distributions.
- Shadows and headlights introduce severe variance.
- Distant vehicles have low pixel density.

## Result

The workflow achieved stable color labeling on test samples and can be integrated into traffic analytics or surveillance dashboards.
