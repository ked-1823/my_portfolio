## Why this project

Clickbait headlines distort content quality and trust. The aim was to automatically flag likely clickbait for moderation or ranking adjustments.

## Modeling strategy

- Constructed a labeled headline dataset.
- Extracted text features capturing intensity and phrasing style.
- Trained classifiers and compared precision-recall tradeoffs.

## Outcome

The model captured recurring clickbait patterns effectively and can support editorial quality control workflows.

## Next steps

- Add transformer-based embeddings.
- Include confidence calibration.
- Evaluate drift over newly collected headlines.
