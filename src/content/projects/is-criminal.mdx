## Use case

Automatically classify news text into criminal vs non-criminal categories for filtering, monitoring, and downstream analytics.

## Approach

- Built a binary classification pipeline for textual data.
- Applied preprocessing and feature extraction consistently.
- Evaluated performance across precision, recall, and F1.

## Results

The model produced reliable baseline predictions and serves as a practical foundation for more granular crime-type taxonomy models.

## Improvement roadmap

- Expand multi-class labeling.
- Add explainability snippets for each prediction.
- Test with region-specific and multilingual news samples.
