## Problem statement

Classify text into sentiment categories so feedback streams can be summarized into actionable business signals.

## Method

- Standardized preprocessing (token cleaning, normalization).
- Feature extraction using classic NLP vectorization.
- Trained and compared baseline classifiers.

## Metrics focus

Instead of accuracy alone, I tracked precision, recall, and F1 to understand class imbalance and model reliability.

## Outcome

The final setup produced consistent sentiment predictions and established a reusable baseline for future transformer upgrades.
