## Project objective

Predict sleep quality score from daily habits (activity, routine timing, and health-related indicators) and provide actionable insights from feature behavior.

## Modeling process

- Performed exploratory analysis for missingness and skew.
- Engineered and normalized predictive features.
- Compared candidate regressors and selected a Random Forest baseline with tuned hyperparameters.

## Outcome

The model delivered reliable predictions on held-out data and was integrated into an API layer for quick experimentation and UI consumption.

## Improvements planned

- Add calibration and uncertainty estimates.
- Expand dataset diversity for better generalization.
- Include explainability outputs (feature importance + SHAP summary).
